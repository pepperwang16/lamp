5. Analysis and Reflections

Observation Insight Design Implication
Users completed all tasks successfully with minimal hesitation. The system is overall easy to learn and efficient. Maintain gesture-based control as primary input.
Occasional confusion with tilt/rotation direction. Gestural cues may be ambiguous without markings. Add visual affordances or physical detents.
Users expressed strong satisfaction with light aesthetics. Ambient feedback enhances perceived usability. Keep the calm visual language in future prototypes.
Desire for more confirmation feedback. Users rely on multimodal confirmation. Consider subtle vibration or tone for the next version.

Both MushLite Orbit and Halo Cube achieved the primary usability goals: high learnability, efficiency, and satisfaction.
