Test Type: Wizard-of-Oz usability evaluation
Participants: 2 users
Duration: ~25 minutes per participant
Setting: Quiet indoor workspace with prototype and facilitator present

Procedure

Introduction (2 mins)

  • Observer records completion time, errors, confusion, comments.

Post-Test Questionnaire (5 mins)

  • Participants complete the post-test questionnaire.

Debrief (1 min)

  • Facilitator thanks the participant and closes session.
